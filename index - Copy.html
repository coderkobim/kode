The XPLora XGO3 is a smartwatch designed for children aged 4 and up. It’s an entry-level device that offers a variety of features to keep kids connected and safe. Here are some of its key features: Voice Calls & Messages: Children can easily reach out to their pre-approved contacts with phone, voice messages, emojis, and chat. GPS & Safety Zones: Parents can track their child’s real-time location and set up safety zones for peace of mind. Activity Platform: The watch encourages kids to be more active by rewarding them with Xplora coins for every thousand steps they take, which can be used on age-appropriate content. Camera: Kids can capture moments with the built-in camera. School Mode: This feature restricts the use of the watch during school hours to minimize distractions. SOS Function: In case of an emergency, children can send an SOS signal to their emergency contacts. The XGO3 comes with an Xplora Connect SIM card, and a monthly subscription plan is required for network connectivity, with prices ranging from $9.99 to $13.99 per month.

The XPLORA X6 Play is a premium smartwatch designed for children aged 6 and up. It offers a range of features to keep kids connected and safe while giving parents peace of mind. Here are some of its key features: Voice Calls & Messages: Allows children to communicate with pre-approved contacts. SOS Function: Kids can send an alert to emergency contacts with their location. GPS & Safety Zones: Parents can track their child’s real-time location and set safety zones. Step Counter & Activity Platform: Encourages physical activity by rewarding steps with Xplora coins. Camera: Enables kids to take photos. WiFi & Network Compatibility: Ensures seamless connectivity. The watch requires a SIM card for network connectivity, with subscription plans starting from $9.99 per month.
